  • About Karuna

“KARUNA FOUNDATION” (KF) was registered in the year 2014 and since then KF is on its mission to lighten up the dark life of the deprived section of the society. Having said that, KF is in the process of bringing them into the mainstream of the society. Considering the moral responsibility on this path of “service to humanity” KF has been carrying out various programs in different fields. The KF team has young, energetic, self motivated, development professionals, who have been contributing their expertise in achieving the goal of this non profit organisation.

  • Events & Updates

covid-19 food relief

Helping families affected with Covid-19, through food packets and with essential ration requirements to meet their daily needs.

happiness kit programme

We always believe that the child that ‘eats better, learns better’ and so we have ensured that the Akshaya patra scheme has been incorporated- by feeding the children along with education.

anganwadi kit programme

Angadwadi as the name suggests ‘courtyard shelter’ similarly Karuna foundation ensures that every child in the rural areas can avail health-care activities including contraceptive counselling and supply, basic nutrition education and supplementation, as well as can enjoy the pre-school activities.

school repopen sanitisation project

Considering the current Covid-19 pandemic, Karuna foundation has also ensured that both the children and the teachers can have the safe and secure execution and functioning of the schools and can benefit from the Mid-Day Meal programme.
