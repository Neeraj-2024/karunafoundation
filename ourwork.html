Program Implementation Thrust Area

Computer Education

Karuna Foundation provides free computer education and training to the underprivileged students. These students are trained by the computer experts who also provide them with the practical training for the hands-on experience. Domain expert trainers and the volunteers ensure that the students receive the best knowledge of the computers.

Help Agriculture People

Karuna Foundation runs programs for farmers and the other stakeholders. Capacity building, livelihood development, watershed development, improvement in farm productivity, increasing the farm income, etc, are some of the popular programs that are managed by KF.

Promote the Computer Education & Education

Apart from only teaching computers, We at KF are also working to boost digital literacy, bridge the digital gaps, facilitate the adoption of computers in education and also equip the youths with the required skills, tools, capabilities that the new economy demands for

Help to student who doing the IIT through scholarship

Karuna Foundation firmly believes that India is a talent hub, and all that it requires is a push in terms of scholarship. Karuna Foundation helps such bright minds, and also impoverished students primarily from the rural regions.

Help to Handicaps

Karuna Foundation put each step ahead in order to help the physically and mentally impaired and the needy people. It brings to us contentment and pleasure as we are achieving things that can not be explained in words, and we want to continue the endeavor towards their development.

Help to Agriculture People (Villages)

With almost more than half of India's population relying on agriculture as a profession, Karuna Foundation as an NGO is looking forward to bringing about a radical change in the agricultural sector. The development of the agricultural sector not only guarantees a healthier economy but also a healthier society.

Arrangement of Govt. Facilities for Agriculture people (villages)

The main objective of ensuring household food security and improving livelihood opportunities and also ensuring overall quality of life for the rural population. This initiative is in collaboration with providing government facilities. Empowering, livelihood support and enhancement, under the government facilities to tribal people in order to access land, and forest products.

Help for construction of school

KF grants help for the construction of school buildings and also for renovation. Most of the students in the school are poor and can not afford education, but since the school helps them to get a good education, we ensure free education to such children, with a school of good infrastructure facilities.

Solve the Problem related to Villages

Karuna Foundation addresses a number of problems related to villages, that includes confronting young people in rural areas, resolving unemployment, marginalisation, agriculture related problems, lack of required resources, poor career prospects, girl child education etc.
