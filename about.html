  • About Karuna

“KARUNA FOUNDATION” (KF) was registered in the year 2014 and since then KF is on its mission to lighten up the dark life of the deprived section of the society. Having said that, KF is in the process of bringing them into the mainstream of the society. Considering the moral responsibility on this path of “service to humanity” KF has been carrying out various programs in different fields. The KF team has young, energetic, self motivated, development professionals, who have been contributing their expertise in achieving the goal of this non profit organisation.

  • Stories of Impact

Promote the Computer Education

Karuna Foundation works in the area of boosting the digital literacy, bridging the gap of digital divide, and also facilitate and promote the adoption of information technologies in the education field for rural public, and equip the disadvantaged youths with the required skill set, tools and capabilities that the new global economy demands for.

Help to Handicaps

As per the survey, the lack of access to special education and one to one attention has led to more than 60,000+ differently abled young students to drop out of the school. Karuna Foundation ensures this and promotes special education for handicapped students.

Arrangement of Free Educations

Karuna Foundation NGO promotes girl child education, and also aims to bring back the missing girls from the classrooms to empower them with equal opportunity through education.

Help for construction of school

Karuna Foundation grants funds for construction or renovation of the school building. Most of the students in the rural school are poor and can not afford education fees, and so these schools provide free education and also help them to get a good education.

  • How we Start

“KARUNA FOUNDATION” (KF) was founded and registered in the year 2014. In hindi language, KARUNA means one that is committed to lighten the dark life of the deprived section of the community and bring them in the mainstream of the society. Considering this as our moral responsibility, the founders of KF went on this path of “service to humanity.” Since then, Karuna Foundation has carried out various programs in different fields. The KF has a team of young, energetic, self motivated, development professionals, who have been contributing their expertise in achieving the goal of the organisation.
